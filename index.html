Never double-posts

Every mix is hashed by content; if that exact audio is already on SoundCloud, it's skipped. Re-render with a change and it counts as new.

One-click or hands-off

Publish selected mixes manually, or let it watch a folder and upload new renders on a schedule.

Manages your library

Lists every track on your account — edit title, tags and genre, toggle public/private, or delete, all in-app.

Your account stays yours

OAuth tokens are stored locally and refreshed automatically; no third-party server ever sees your audio.
